Who we are
We understand where the gaps are before most of the market sees them.

Most traders in this market work on handshakes and phone calls. That works — until two reliable buyers or sellers go quiet, the season shifts, or a deal falls through with no backup plan.

Stone Peak Trading was built on a different foundation. We study production cycles, supply constraints, demand patterns and grain movement across Southern and Eastern Africa and global markets. We move on opportunities before they become obvious.

That is how we trade on our own account. It is the same system we bring into every consulting engagement and everything we build.
