Active Trading — Stone Peak Trading

We don't broker deals. We execute them.

Stone Peak Trading is an active physical commodity trading company operating across Southern and East African markets. We source, structure and execute trades on our own account — connecting serious buyers and sellers across grains, pulses, oilseeds, sugar and fertilizer.

How we trade
01
We trade on our own account
Stone Peak Trading takes principal positions. We buy and sell physical commodities using our own capital and network — we are not a brokerage and we do not earn commissions on deals we don't own.
02
We move product across the region
Our operations span Southern and East Africa. We understand the logistics, the documentation, the financing and the counterparty relationships required to move physical commodity volumes reliably across borders.
03
We build long term supply relationships
We are not interested in one-off transactions. Our model is built on consistent, documented supply and offtake relationships — with producers, processors, exporters and importers who need a reliable trading partner.
